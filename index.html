The Infernal Chronicles

Chapter 1: The Reassignment

Your performance review is in, and it's not good. Demoted from the comfort of Soul Retention to the cutthroat war zone of Acquisitions, you find yourself reporting to a new manager: Mathias Segan. He's arrogant, manipulative, and the very demon who ruined your career. Welcome to your new personal hell. Try not to get fired (literally).

Chapter 2: The Rose

Is it a peace offering, or a trap? You return home to find a single red rose on your doormat. But in Hell, gifts rarely come without strings attached. A confrontation with Mathias turns into a lesson in punctuality as he opens a portal to the mortal realm. The sun is up, the clock is ticking, and your training has officially begun.

Chapter 3: The Hunter

Mathias takes you to a coastal village to teach you the difference between a scavenger and a collector. While lesser demons fight over scraps, he has his eyes on a bigger prize: a pious monk with a dark secret. It's a masterclass in manipulation, but you quickly realize that when you hunt big game, you're rarely the only predator in the field.

Chapter 4: The Seraphim

Holy ground burns, but the Angel standing on it burns brighter. You come face-to-face with Archer, a Seraphim in disguise, who is running interference for your target. Chapter 5: The Choice

Back in the Pit, the silence of Mathias's office is deafening. He knows you spoke to the Angel. He demands to know what was said. You hold the truth that could save a soul or cement your career. Do you betray Archer's trust to impress your boss, or do you lie to the most dangerous demon in Acquisitions?
